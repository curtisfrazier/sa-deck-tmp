PREDICTION 1 / 5
The Trillion-Dollar Cluster

“Every six months, another zero gets added to the plans.”

He predicted · 2024
  • $10B → $100B → trillion-dollar super-clusters.
  • "Trillions of dollars" of total AI infrastructure spending.
  • Single sites scaling toward gigawatts of power.
What happened · by 2026
  • Stargate: $500B project unveiled at the White House (Jan 2025).
  • Big Tech capex guided to ~$725B in 2026 alone.
  • xAI "Colossus": ~555,000 GPUs, 2 GW, Memphis.
  • Meta building a 5 GW site ("Hyperion").
✓ CONFIRMEDDirection nailed — though 100 GW clusters are still ahead.

PREDICTION 2 / 5
Power Becomes The Binding Constraint

The bottleneck isn't chips. It's electricity.

He predicted · 2024
  • Electricity becomes the hard limit on AI growth.
  • U.S. needs "tens of percent" more power generation.
  • A scramble for nuclear, gas — any available power.
What happened · by 2026
  • Three Mile Island restarting to power Microsoft (Sep 2024).
  • Amazon, Google & Meta sign nuclear deals — Meta up to 6.6 GW.
  • xAI runs ~35 gas turbines on-site in Memphis.
  • Data-center demand surges; power bills rising.
✓ CONFIRMEDPerhaps the paper's most accurate call.

STAYING HONEST
What Hasn't Happened — Yet

A fair forecast deserves honest grading.

  • AGI hasn't arrived. "2027" is still a forecast — and a hotly contested one.
  • Clusters are single-digit gigawatts, not the ~100 GW he projected for 2030.
  • AI revenue is ~$20–30B, not the $100B+ trajectory's endpoint.
  • "Bubble" warnings are loud. Timelines could slip; spending could correct.
The direction is unmistakable. The destination and timing are still uncertain.
